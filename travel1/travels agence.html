Merzouga Desert Erg Chebbi Trip



Small village in southeastern Morocco, Merzouga owes its renown to the high dunes of Erg Chebbi which extends to its edge. Merzouga’s dunes are a popular destination for travelers seeking a complete immersion in the heart of a Manifique desert environment!



Marrakech City



Marrakech has the most beautiful gardens including gardens created by a famous French painter. This is where the people who stay in the mountains and the travelers meet as one hears it as a place of great importance, a must to see. If you are looking for serenity, you should visit the Koutoubia Mosque.


kitesurfing holidays in Essaouira, Morocco


Essaouira is an enchanting port city facing the Atlantic Ocean, known as a great surfing destination with strong coastal winds. With amazing surf spots as Safi Beach and Essaouira Beach, these sandy breaks are perfect for beginners and intermediate surfers.




Au top de l’Afrique du Nord !


Nous vous emmenons à plus de 4000 mètres d’altitude au sommet le plus élevé de toute l’Afrique du Nord ! Rando grand style avec convoi de mules, guides confirmés, cuisiniers tout-terrain et nuits en refuge afin d’atteindre le sommet du mont Toubkal dans le Haut-Atlas. Que ce tu défi .




Sightseeing and Staying in Al Hoceima


Favorite places to visit in the city include Mimoun El Arssi Stadium, Quemado Beach, and Al Hoceima Marina. Additionally, you can explore the sights and enjoy the activities in the larger area.
Visitors can enjoy all that Al Hoceima has to offer including its beaches.
